## 4. Inventory Governance

- The full list is a product map, not an instruction to build all pages.
- A page is activated only after its business process and permissions are validated.
- Multiple pages may be implemented as tabs or drawers when validation shows they belong to one task.
- Database tables must not be generated directly from page count.
- Page names, role visibility, and required actions are the source for subsequent screen breakdown.


## 5. Test XLM Page Consolidation

`AQT-PG-03-01 — Test XLM` is the only top-level White Belt page for wallet, balance, and transaction actions. The remaining wallet-related page records are future detail views or may become internal sections rather than independent navigation items after user validation.


## 6. Scope Vocabulary

- **White Belt Active:** required for the current Level 1 submission.
- **Future Business MVP:** product workflow to validate after White Belt; not a current implementation requirement.
- **Contract / Belt Progression:** requires smart-contract or later-level scope.
- **P2 / Scale:** later operational expansion.

`Payment Summary` belongs to Future Business MVP. It must not duplicate the active Test XLM send form or appear as a second White Belt payment menu.
