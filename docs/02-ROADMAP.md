# Product Roadmap — AquaTrade Pay

## Phase 1 — White Belt

Wallet, balance, and direct Testnet XLM payment.

## Phase 2 — Trade foundation

Introduce real domain entities and off-chain workflow after the blueprint is approved.

## Phase 3 — Verifiable evidence

Add document references and tamper-evident proof design.

## Phase 4 — Stellar smart contract

Integrate the independently versioned `aquatrade-contracts` repository.

## Phase 5 — Operational workflow

Add shipment and domain-specific verification flows.

## Phase 6 — Claims and dispute

Add explicit state transitions, resolution rules, and audit evidence.

## Phase 7 — Mainnet and traction

Proceed only after mentor technical and market-fit checkpoints.
